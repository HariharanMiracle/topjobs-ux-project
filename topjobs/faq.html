FAQ


What is topjobs?

topjobs is an online recruitment system used by employers to list their vacancies online and also handle online applications from jobseekers. topjobs completes and automates the entire recruitment cycle, and gives an electronic 'back-office' to make recruitment easy, fast and efficient.


How can I benefit from using topjobs?

If you are a job seeker, looking for your first job, or considering a career change, then topjobs is perfect for you! You can browse or search for vacancies and apply online immediately ... Your application will be sent straight-through to the HR team of topjobs registered employers. They will contact you if you're shortlisted. If you are an employer, then topjobs will give you a complete and comprehensive electronic recruitment platform, for online vacancy advertising, and for automating the recruitment process in a totally paperless environment. Registered employers get their own topjobs Home Page where they can list vacancies and also provide information on the benefits of working for the company. Read More >>

How does the employer see my information and my application?

When you apply for a vacancy, you can do two things to present your application to the employer. First, you can upload your CV, and other documents such as application ... cover letter and photographs, testimonials etc. So applying through topjobs is no different to a traditional application - by email or post - but the difference is that you will benefit from the additional features of the topjobs recruitment system - such as straight through processing, and employer feedback information etc. Your application is stored immediately in a database, so there is no chance of getting lost in the post, or getting blocked by a spam filter. Secondly, you can fill out a standardised application profile that has been customised by each employer. Although filling out a profile is optional, it highly reccommended, because the employer will be using the profile to compare you with other applicants. So please put as much information as you can into your profile. Read More >>

Is it absolutely necessary to fill in all the sections of the profile?

It is not absolutely necessary to fill in all the sections. However it is highly advisable that you fill in important sections such as Education, Qualifications and Professional ... Experience, to clearly show the employer the information which is vital to evaluate your application. The system uses the information in the profile to do candidate matching, and ranking, and there are also several comparision reports in the employer's recruitment back-office that compares candidates against each other using the profiles. So fill out the profile to the best of your ability to improve the quality of your application. Read More >>
